CITY SUP WALKS
Pushkino has several locations within the city: the Serebryanka River and the Ucha River. Both routes are suitable for everyone, without exception, both children and adults, during the entire SUP season. Our instructor will conduct training before the walk and will accompany you during it. The duration of the walk is from 1 hour to 1.5 - 2 hours.
Sap walks outside the city
River Ucha in Ivanteevka - this route is suitable for walks for both adults and children in May, June, July. Pestovskoye Reservoir in Tishkovo - the large water of this place attracts with its scale, beautiful sunsets, yachts, waves from jet skis. Walks are possible during the whole SUP season.Vyaz River in Tishkovo - this route is available in May, June, later algae become an obstacle. Suitable for adults and children.
SURISES AND SUNSETS
Our team regularly publishes the time of walks when you can see amazing sunrises on the Serebryanka River and sunsets on the Ucha River (or on some days you can watch sunsets in Tishkovo). Follow the schedule!
RENTAL / RENTAL OF SUPBOARDS
Top SUP boards are available for self-rental. When taking the boards, we provide life jackets, explain how to use them correctly, and provide detailed instructions for using the boards. When renting on your own, you choose a place for walking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you often ask what to wear on a SUP board?

And you can just wear comfortable clothes. For example: leggings and a T-shirt, or shorts and a T-shirt. If the weather is cold, take a windbreaker. Wear comfortable sneakers, but if it's hot outside, you can go barefoot on the board! And don't forget to take a change of clothes in case you fall into the water, yes, it happens!

What is a self-guided SUP board rental?

Renting allows you to choose any place and any duration of the walk. Self-renting means that you arrive at the address, leave a deposit of 20 tr. In exchange for a set: a backpack, a deflated board, a fin, a pump, an oar. You yourself inflate / deflate the board and return it in its pure form after an independent walk.

Where do you put your stuff while walking?

You can always leave your things in our car. Or we can offer you a special hermetic bag and things will walk on the water with you!

Can my 120kg husband come for a SUP ride?

Everyone weighing up to 150 kg can walk on a SUP!

How many people can come to you for a walk at the same time?

You can come to us in large groups of up to 10 people!

I've never tried supboard, do you teach?

Don't be afraid to try! Before each walk, our experienced instructor will conduct a detailed briefing and will accompany you on the water. Each rider is given a life jacket.

Can I bring children with me?

A child of 3-11 years old rides with you on the same sap. Children over 11 ride on their own. Let's put on SUP before the walk. We issue a life jacket of a suitable size. The instructor will be nearby during the entire ride.
